About Rebound

Our mission and commitment to beauty transparency

Rebound is an AI-powered beauty recommendation platform designed to help you discover skincare products that match your skin type, concerns, and budget. Our mobile app delivers curated results based on your quiz input and product attributes. We're committed to transparency, data ethics, and providing meaningful recommendations — not just ads.

After years of testing products that didn't work for our team's diverse skin types and concerns, we built Rebound to address the core problem: beauty advice doesn't account for individual differences. What works for one person won't necessarily work for you.

Our platform analyzes thousands of verified user reviews, ingredient lists, and product claims to match you with products that have worked for people with skin profiles similar to yours. We provide clear, honest assessments of each recommendation, including potential drawbacks and personalized compatibility scores.
