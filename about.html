About Us

Why we built Rebound — and who it’s for

Founder Story

Rebound is owned and operated by Ike Jeon, a technology professional with deep experience building products. Like many people, he cared about having clear, healthy skin but often felt lost about where to begin. That search led him to a community of others—including experts—who shared the same concerns and questions about ingredients for different skin types. Drawing on his background in the tech industry, Ike built Rebound to make skincare simple and transparent through personalized, ingredient-aware recommendations.

Mission — Why We Exist

We believe everyone deserves clear, trustworthy guidance—not marketing hype—when it comes to skincare.

What We Do

Rebound analyzes ingredients, real reviews, and product claims to recommend skincare that actually matches your skin type, concerns, and budget.

What Makes Us Different

  • Transparent ingredient insights (no hidden ads)
  • AI-powered enrichment that stays up to date
  • Affordable, personalized recommendations

Community & Trust

We’ve discovered a growing community of people—consumers and professionals—who care about ingredients and results. Rebound is built for them, with them.
